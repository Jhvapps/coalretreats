---

## 🚫 HARD RULES (Brand Tone)

**Never use these words/angles:**
- ❌ "cheap", "cosy", "budget", "deal", "discount", "affordable"
- ❌ "cabin", "rental", "Airbnb-style"
- ❌ Party / backpacker / individual-room framing

**Always lean into:**
- ✅ Private, premium, experience-led, basecamp, group, retreat
- ✅ Space, privacy, quality, understated luxury
- ✅ Food-led, host-led, year-round

**Pricing language:** premium for the Pyrenees, underpriced vs Alps. Never discount. Never run "deals."
