# Coal Retreats — Outreach Plan

**Goal:** Fill the chalet 40+ weeks/year. Premium-price bookings, recurring guests.
**Capacity:** 14 pax, £10,000+/week catered. Break-even goal: ~£520k/yr.
**Unique sell:** Private-hire, uncrowded alternative to the big Alpine resorts. Ski + bike + wellness on one site.

---

## 🎯 TARGET AUDIENCES (by priority)

### 1. Groups of friends (30-55, UK & Europe)
- **Who:** Friend groups with disposable income, planning annual ski/bike trip
- **Hook:** "Skip the Courchevel queues — your own house in the Pyrenees"
- **Avg booking:** £10-15k week

### 2. Corporate retreats / off-sites (B2B)
- **Who:** 10-14 person teams, UK tech/finance/agency
- **Hook:** 14-person capacity, home cinema, break-out spaces, private chef for team dinners, away from a hotel
- **Avg booking:** £15-20k week

### 3. Cycling clubs & Tour de France chasers
- **Who:** Ambitious amateur cyclists, especially July-September
- **Hook:** 4 legendary cols within striking distance (Peyresourde, Aspin, Tourmalet, Azet)

### 4. Multi-gen families
- **Who:** 10-14 pax family, grandparents → toddlers
- **Hook:** Families page already built — lake on doorstep, low-altitude kid-friendly skiing at Peyragudes

### 5. Weddings / milestone celebrations
- **Who:** Intimate 30-50 pax parties with 14 sleeping
- **Hook:** Exclusive use, private chef, scenic

## 💡 POSITIONING / KEY MESSAGES

- **"Hidden, uncrowded, genuinely beautiful"** (current site tagline — reinforce everywhere)
- **"Your basecamp, not a hotel"** — exclusive-use is the differentiator
- **"Pyrenees, not Alps"** — lean into the anti-Courchevel angle
- **"Ski Peyragudes in 9 minutes"** — proximity beats prestige
- **"14 beds, one door"** — for corporate / group messaging
- **"Tour de France cols on the doorstep"** — for cycling messaging

---

## ⚠️ WATCH OUTS

- **Don't compete on price** with AirBnB — lead on experience (chef, concierge)
- **Respond within 4 hours** — group enquiries are high-intent; slow replies kill deals
- **Book direct discount** — once agents prove demand, incentivise direct bookings (~8% discount, saves the 10-15% commission)
- **Seasonal pricing** — December peak, January mid, February half-term peak, March shoulder, summer peak July-August
