Color · Hierarchy

Three colors carry the brand

Amber is the signature — warm, low-light, alpine evening. Pine grounds it in the valley. Charcoal is the reading surface. Cream is the paper everything sits on.

Amber — Primary

#C8842A

The signature. CTAs, highlights, accents in dark sections, hero moments. Never on white text for body; always against cream, charcoal or photography.

Pine — Secondary

#2D4A35

The valley. Summer sections, nature context, large colour fields, quiet backgrounds. Pairs with amber for depth.

Charcoal — Tertiary

#1C1A18

Body copy. Dark navigation. Night-scene backgrounds. Never pure black — always this warm near-black.

Cream — Surface

#FAF6EF

The paper. Every page, every section, every print piece sits on cream. Not white.

"Cream is the stillness. Amber is the lantern in the window. Pine is the forest around it. Charcoal is the night outside."

Principles

Six rules shape every decision

Design and copy both answer to the same north star — calm, understated luxury for groups who value space, privacy, and experience over trophies.

01

Private only

Exclusive use, every booking. Never a shared space, never a room-by-room sell. The whole property, or not at all.

02

Space over saturation

Photography, copy, and layout all breathe. More white space than content. Let the mountains and the architecture do the talking.

03

Food-led

Dining is the centre of the stay, not a footnote. Private chef, Pyrenean produce, long dinners. Treat food moments with the same weight as hero imagery.

04

Basecamp, not hotel

Guests return from adventure to warmth, not to a front desk. Every touchpoint should feel like a home that's been prepared for them.

05

Year-round, never seasonal

Winter and summer carry equal weight. Ski imagery and cycling imagery appear in proportion. Autumn and spring are opportunities, not gaps.

06

Calm premium, never loud

Understated, editorial, confident. No superlatives, no exclamation marks, no "ultra-luxury" language. The quality is the claim.

Voice — say this

  • Private, exclusive-use, basecamp
  • Space, privacy, quality, calm
  • Food-led, host-led, experience-led
  • Mountain, valley, retreat, group
  • Year-round, year after year

Voice — never

  • Cheap, cosy, budget, affordable
  • Deal, discount, special offer
  • Cabin, rental, Airbnb-style
  • Party, wild, epic, ultimate
  • Exclamation marks. Ever.
