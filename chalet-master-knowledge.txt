# CHALET MASTER KNOWLEDGE FILE
# Project: Coal Retreats Chalet
# Location: Avajan, Louron Valley, French Pyrenees

--------------------------------------------------
1. CORE ASSET OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------

Name: Coal Retreats Chalet (working brand)
Type: Private, exclusive-use luxury chalet
Location: Avajan, Louron Valley, French Pyrenees (near Loudenvielle)
Capacity: Sleeps up to 14 guests
Rooms: 6 en-suite bedrooms
Use Type: Entire property only (no room-by-room rentals)

Property Origin:
- Fully renovated 1880s post house
- High-quality carpentry and alpine character
- Designed for group experiences, not transient stays

Positioning:
- Premium, private mountain retreat
- Food-led, experience-led
- Designed for groups (not individuals or couples)

Tone:
- Calm, understated luxury
- No "cheap", "cosy", "budget" language
- Focus on space, privacy, quality, experience

--------------------------------------------------
2. BUSINESS MODEL
--------------------------------------------------

Model Type:
- Exclusive-use weekly and short-stay bookings
- High-value group stays

Operational Phases:
- Phase 1 (until May 1): Self-catered
- Phase 2 (after May 1): Fully catered / hosted chalet
- Self-catered remains possible at premium pricing

Revenue Strategy:
- Fewer bookings, higher value
- Target high spend per group
- Focus on margin over occupancy

Target Revenue:
- High average booking value
- Repeat and referral driven

--------------------------------------------------
3. TARGET MARKET
--------------------------------------------------

Primary Segments:

1. Adult Ski Groups
- UK and European groups
- Premium but not ultra-luxury Alps pricing
- Want ease, privacy, and quality

2. Cycling & Adventure Groups
- Summer season (huge opportunity)
- Road cycling, mountain biking, hiking
- Returning customers likely

3. Corporate / Professional Groups
- Offsites, strategy retreats, team building
- Founders, leadership teams, SMEs
- Private environment = key selling point

4. Families (Secondary)
- Multi-generational stays
- School holidays
- Activity-led

NOT targeting:
- Budget travellers
- Party groups
- Individual room bookings
- Backpackers

--------------------------------------------------
4. LOCATION ADVANTAGE
--------------------------------------------------

Area: Louron Valley / Loudenvielle

Key Strengths:
- Underrated vs Alps (less crowded, better value)
- Rapidly developing destination
- Year-round appeal

Winter:
- Peyragudes ski resort (via Skyvall gondola)
- Val Louron nearby
- Fast access from valley floor

Summer:
- Tour de France cycling territory
- World-class climbs
- Hiking, lakes, outdoor sports

Major Attractions:
- Skyvall Gondola (fast lift access)
- Lac de Génos-Loudenvielle (lake, activities)
- Balnéa Spa (thermal wellness centre)
- Bike parks & pump tracks
- Adventure parks

Trend:
- Increasing investment in region
- Growing international awareness
- Moving toward premium tourism

6. PRICING STRATEGY
--------------------------------------------------

Principle:
- Premium for the Pyrenees market
- Underpriced vs Alps but positioned high-end locally

Indicative Pricing (Self-Catered Phase):
- Week (standard): €4,000–€4,500
- Peak weeks: €5,000–€5,500
- Weekends: €1,500–€1,700
- Midweek: €1,000–€1,200

Future (Catered Model):
- Significantly higher pricing
- Focus on per-person or full-package pricing

Rules:
- Avoid discounting
- Avoid "deals"
- Maintain perceived value
