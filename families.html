All-weather days,
sorted.

The largest thermal spa complex in the Pyrenees, and it welcomes families. Rain, cold, tired legs? This is the answer.

  • Kids lagoon
  • Warm indoor & outdoor pools
  • Relaxed, family-friendly atmosphere

Skiing & Gondola

Straight to the
mountain.

The Skyvall gondola runs directly from Loudenvielle village up to Peyragudes. No driving, no parking, no stress, just ski-in convenience for the whole family.

  • Direct gondola from the village
  • Beginner-friendly pistes & ski school
  • Summer mountain trips & hiking
  • 9 minutes door to lift

Bike Parks & Pump Track

Burn off energy
in the village.

The Louron Valley is a cyclist's playground at every level. For the kids, there's a proper pump track and easy valley trails that even the smallest riders can enjoy.

  • Pump track in the village
  • Bike hire on-site
  • Easy family trails & greenways
  • Lift-accessed mountain biking for older kids
